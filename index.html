Hello! I'm a postdoc with Dorothee Kern at Brandeis University. I'm interested in using deep learning to improve how we design enzymes.

I did my PhD in Chemistry at Stanford, where I used deep learning to better parse protein dynamics with Vijay Pande, and improved RNA structure prediction and stabilized RNA therapeutics with Rhiju Das.

Prior, I studied theoretical chemistry at Cambridge University as a Churchill scholar, and chemistry and mathematics at Pomona College.

When not thinking about science, I can most often be found in a boat of some sort.
