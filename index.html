Hello! I'm a [Jane Coffin Childs](https://www.jccfund.org/) postdoctoral fellow with [Dorothee Kern](https://kernlab-brandeis.github.io/) at Brandeis University. I'm interested in using deep learning to improve how we design enzymes.

I did my PhD in Chemistry at Stanford, where I improved RNA structure prediction and stabilized RNA therapeutics with [Rhiju Das](https://daslab.stanford.edu/), and used deep learning to better parse protein dynamics with Vijay Pande.

Before that, I studied theoretical chemistry at Cambridge University as a [Churchill scholar](https://www.churchillscholarship.org/), and chemistry and mathematics at Pomona College.

When not thinking about science, I can most often be found in a boat of some sort.
